The Corner Spheres Puzzle

Why does the central sphere burst out of the cube?

FROM THE VIDEO
120
CENTRAL RADIUS
1.00
In n dimensions the distance from center to a corner of the unit hypercube is √n. Central sphere radius = √n − 1. When this exceeds 1, the sphere protrudes the cube faces.

Why Volume Approaches Zero

In high dimensions almost all volume lives in a razor-thin shell near the surface.

At n=100 the volume is ~2.37×10⁻⁴⁰ — effectively nothing compared to the containing hypercube.
